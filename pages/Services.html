Our Services

At Afya Mama, we provide a range of specialized services designed to support mothers and children throughout their health journeys. Our offerings are tailored to promote well-being, provide essential resources, and empower families with knowledge and care.

Prenatal and Postnatal Care

Comprehensive support and guidance for mothers before and after childbirth, including health check-ups, nutritional guidance, and counseling.

Child Immunization

Ensuring that children receive all essential vaccinations on schedule to protect against common diseases and promote healthy development.

Maternal Health Education

Access to educational resources covering topics like breastfeeding, maternal nutrition, and safe parenting practices.

Nutrition Counseling

Personalized dietary advice for mothers and children to meet their nutritional needs at every stage of growth and development.

Mental Health Support

Confidential counseling and mental health support services tailored to the unique challenges of motherhood and child-rearing.

Community Health Workshops

Interactive workshops designed to engage, educate, and empower communities on maternal and child health topics.
